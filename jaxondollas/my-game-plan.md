# My Game Plan

## Project Name 
Coin Collector

## What It Does 
Players run around a map collecting coins. The player With the most after 60 seconds wins.

## Features 
- A coin spawner that places 10 coins around the map 
- A score counter for each player 
- A 60 second timer 
- A winner announcment at the end 

## How to Play
1. Open the game in **Roblox Studio** and press Play 
2. Walk into yellow coins to collect them Watch your score go up 
3. See who wins when the timer runs out
